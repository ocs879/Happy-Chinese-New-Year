About us

"TakoDonald® always serve the best and fresh ingredients to their beloved customers." -DailyNews

TakoDonald® is a Mexican based taco restaurant which has many franchises in around Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Mexico and United State America. TakoDonald® has been serving their customers for over 24 years. TakoDonald® has varied types of Mexican foods in their menu.

"If it is possible, I hope my taco can bring every one happiness. We, the TakoDonald®, will serve the freshest ingredients for making tacos to all our customers." -Tom Donald





TakoDonald's Story



1996

The story of Tom Donald and Richard Mashimoto:


The first TakoDonald® restaurant started in Merida, Mexico in the early of 1996 by two successful businessmen, who are Tom Donald and Richard Mashimoto. They bought appliances for their small taco restaurant from salesman Charlie Santiago, who was intrigued by their need for deep fryers and soft drinks machine.




1999

Franchises throughout the worldwide:


They decided to expand their business as they have a good income throughout the months and years. Their target countries are Mexico, United State America, and Asia.




2003

The iconic TakoDonald® logo:


After 4 years, the logo of TakoDonald® has announced it had been created and registered officially, and it becomes an iconic logo throughout the worldwide. And they added different types of taco shells, flavors, and toppings inside their menu, and it has become a trend that customers can choose ingredients and customize their own taco with their own likes.




2010

Successful Businessmen:


In 2010, they have received “The Best Businessmen” and “Iconic Company” award. After the hard working for 14 years, Tom Donald and Richard Mashimoto have gained their respect and reputation while running the business of TakoDonald® restaurant.
